Technical Specifications

Dimensions & Physical Properties

Standard Length 600mm
Standard Height 200mm / 250mm
Thickness Options 4", 6", 8", 9" (100-225mm)
Density 550-650 kg/m³

Performance

Compressive Strength 3.0 - 4.5 N/mm²
Thermal Conductivity 0.16 - 0.18 W/mK
Sound Reduction ~45dB (200mm wall)
Fire Rating 4+ hours (200mm wall)

Material & Composition

Materials Cement, Fly Ash, Lime, Gypsum
Manufacturing Process Autoclaved (High-Pressure Steam)
Air Pore Content ~80% of volume
Color Light Grey

Installation & Performance

Mortar Requirement Thin-bed Mortar (2-3mm)
Water Absorption < 15% by weight
Drying Shrinkage < 0.04%
Warranty 10 Years (Structural)

Advantages

Why AAC blocks are the smarter choice for modern construction

Energy Savings

Reduces heating/cooling costs by up to 30% compared to traditional brick or concrete structures.

Eco-Friendly

Uses recycled industrial waste (fly ash) and requires less energy to produce than traditional building materials.

Faster Construction

Larger size and lightweight nature speed up construction by up to 50% compared to conventional brick masonry.

Easy Workability

Can be easily cut, shaped, and drilled with conventional tools, allowing for precise fitting and reduced waste.

Pest Resistant

Inorganic material that does not support mold growth and is naturally resistant to pests and termites.

Sound Insulation

Porous structure absorbs sound waves, creating quieter interior spaces and reducing noise pollution.

Applications

Versatile building material for a wide range of construction needs

Residential Buildings

Perfect for energy-efficient homes, offering superior insulation and comfort while reducing ongoing energy costs.

Commercial Structures

Ideal for offices, hotels, and retail spaces, providing excellent fire resistance and acoustic properties.

Interior Partitions

Creates sound-dampening interior walls with excellent fire ratings and smooth surfaces for finishing.
